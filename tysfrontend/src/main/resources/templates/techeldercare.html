TechElderCare

​

TechYourSpace is an online curator of smart products that automate living spaces like home | office | public spaces (restaurants, malls, etc)

The key USP of TechYourSpace is the virtual boutique experience. Users will be to walkthrough virtual rooms to explore new products placed in home or office spaces.

The motive is:

  • Product should add value to TechYourSpace platform
  • Product should ultimately enhance the life experience of users (in terms of quality)
  • To have a balance between products for senior citizens vs millennial and kids
  • The should fit into the concept of website's boutique experience and virtual reality walkthrough
